About oceans

Guardians of Life on Earth

Oceans cover more than 70% of Earth's surface and represent the most important ecosystem on our planet. They regulate climate, produce oxygen, and provide home to millions of species.

Each ocean has its own unique story — from the icy waters of the Arctic to warm tropical currents. We invite you to discover them.
